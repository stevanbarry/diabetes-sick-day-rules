Insulin with Food

Take your insulin as normal. Take carbohydrates as a meal replacement and sip sugarfree liquids (at least 100 mL/hour if you are able)

If you start vomiting, are unable to keep fluids down or are unable to control your blood glucose or ketone levels, you must seek urgent medical advice.

DO NOT STOP TAKING YOUR INSULIN EVEN IF YOU ARE NOT ABLE TO EAT
